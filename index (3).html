Project 3: PPF Visualizer

Project Overview

The Production Possibility Frontier (PPF) shows the maximum combinations of two goods that a country can produce using its available resources and technology. It helps explain scarcity, choice, and opportunity cost.

What I Learned

I learned that resources are limited, so producing more of one good usually means producing less of another. The PPF helps visualize these trade-offs and opportunity costs.
