Project 1: Comparative Advantage Simulator

Project Overview

Comparative advantage is a key concept in international trade. It explains why countries specialize in producing goods with lower opportunity costs and then trade with other countries. This specialization increases efficiency and allows all trading partners to benefit.

Demonstration

Select which country should specialize in each product.

  • Country A: Lower opportunity cost for Cloth
  • Country B: Lower opportunity cost for Wine

Result: Country A specializes in Cloth and Country B specializes in Wine.

Evidence

The table below summarizes the production capabilities used in the Comparative Advantage analysis.

Country Cloth (units) Wine (units)
Country A 100 50
Country B 80 120

I learned that countries do not need to be the best at producing everything. By specializing in products with lower opportunity costs and trading with other countries, both countries can achieve higher overall welfare.
