Project 5: Trade Pattern Analyzer

Project Overview

Trade patterns explain why countries export certain products and import others. These patterns are influenced by comparative advantage, factor endowments, technology, resources, and consumer demand.

Demonstration

Electronics 500
Textiles 300
Machinery 150

What I Learned

I learned that trade patterns are not random. Countries usually export goods that make use of their abundant resources and import goods that require resources they lack.
