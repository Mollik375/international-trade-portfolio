Project 2: Gravity Model Explorer

Project Overview

The Gravity Model of Trade explains why some countries trade more with each other than others. According to the model, countries with larger economies tend to trade more, while greater distance between countries reduces trade.

Demonstration

Trade tends to increase with GDP and decrease with distance.

Country Pair Trade Potential
USA - Canada High
USA - Australia Medium

What I Learned

I learned that international trade is influenced by both economic size and geographic distance. Countries with larger economies and closer locations usually trade more with each other.
